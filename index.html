About Me

I am a Junior Front-end Web developer based in South Africa. My passion lies in using technology for social change, building things that matter, and a better world, like creating innovative technologies, creating systems, or products that are useful and that have economic benefits in the digital economy.

I love what I do, and I am passionate about coding, and it makes me to challenge myself on a daily basis. It is the driving force behind what I do. It is my motivation to strive to improve with every project. Coding has for years been a male dominated field, however there are more women in programming who are making significant contributions to the field. This knowledge has taught me the importance of having the perseverance to change things. Therefore, I will strive towards making positive changes.

By adapting and being patient in solving complex problems,I have developed multiple skills and learnt to find solutions.It is my hope that my successes and achievements will further motivate more women to consider this field. It would be a great priviledge and pleasure if I could make direct and meaningful contributions to the empowerment of women.

I also love design because it helps me dream up creative concepts that take advantage of my surroundings for extraordinary results, creating beautiful artwork and lettering. I find painting and lettering to be both fun and also incredibly therapeutic, allowing me to calm my mind by focusing on each pen or brush stroke.

I am an Ada graduate

I have studied front-end development at the African Developers Academy (ADA). The program encourages an environment of support and empathy that contributes to the success of their students. The learning space has been a truly collaborative environment,It was an invaluable experience and I really enjoyed it

I made mistakes and learnt from them - they have helped me to grow as a person. Working in groups has taught me the importance of teamwork, and how much my contributions can add value. I am willing to go the extra mile in order to achieve the objectives and goals. I learnt to put more effort into my job and my projects.

As a graduate from ADA, programming languages have affected the way I think about things. I am confident that the skills I have acquired during the learnership program will help me in my endeavour to become an exceptional coder. The journey has just begun but I am looking forward to new and exciting challenges ahead.
