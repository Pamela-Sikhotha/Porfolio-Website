About me

Junior Front-End Developer

Skills and Passions

I am an avid learner of all things front-end development and I have learned to code in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I continue learning in my free time using the Free Code Camp website. One of my passions is creating beautiful artwork and lettering. I am a self-taught watercolouring artist and I love to spend time drawing in my journal, creating paintings of still life’s and abstract artwork. I am also very good at Inkscape, Gimp and Canva which I use them to create wireframes and manipulate images for new web development projects. I am also a multi-instrumentalist and love to play music at church and for my family during my leisure time. I also love reading, It helps me step into the imaginative world and experience a whole new world.

My future goals

I have an insatiable desire to develop and grow my coding skills. Software development is exciting and challenging but I’m ready and excited to pursue my goals. I have so many ideas I would like to use my skills on but my first priority is that I hope to inspire others to achieve their dreams regardless of whether or not they look-the-part. It’s important for people to have role models that look more like them in positions that they aspire to be in. I hope to show aspiring developers that despite the current demographic in STEM fields, they should not be deterred in their ambitions.
