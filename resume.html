About me

I am an ADA graduate who loves all things tech and digital, from designing websites to developing them. My research interest involves user experience, creativity, and graphic design.

I have a passion for software development, I have been formally trained and invested time into learning different programming languages such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript at ADA. I have been able to quickly develop my skills through the help of my lecturers, self-study, and leveraging resources. I have worked on different projects and cannot wait to tackle my next one. I am curious and want to learn and implement new technologies. I have the ability to think creatively, analytically, and with depth about problems.

Community is incredibly important to me. I love working with others and care about constructive feedback since it's an important part of working with people and I am able to both give and receive feedback. I am a young, open-minded woman who strives to learn, grow, and who is engaged and involved in everything I set my mind to. I have the ability to work through obstacles despite setbacks and failures like now experiencing the COVID-19 pandemic at hand since it deterred plans for the future but I continue to be positive. I am willing to go the extra mile, and I always do things to the best of my ability.

I have the ability to adapt to changing situations and cope with stress, I am also adept at clearly communicating my needs and asking for help. I am always willing to commit myself to all aspects of software development as well as embrace the values of community, inclusivity, and lifelong learning.

I have acquired social and life skills; communication, time management, analytical skills and independent learning. I understand my role as a team player, and I am capable to complete a product life cycle from conception to delivery and I can utilize agile practices learned to assist in project completion. I have obtained skills in the following coding languages: HTML, CSS and JavaScript; which are the basic building blocks for front-end web development. Programming languages affect the way you think about things and are well worth knowing. The knowledge I acquired from the African Developers Academy has helped me become an exceptional coder. I have an insatiable desire to develop and grow my coding skills. Software development is exciting and challenging but I’m ready and excited to pursue my goals.

Passions and skills

I am a devoted coder and unconventional web-designer at heart. I acquired skills in the following coding languages: HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

I am proficient at using design platforms such as Gimp, InkScape and Canva to design wireframes and manipulate images for all web development projects. I am a zealous creative.

One of my passions is creating beautiful artwork and lettering. I am a self-taught watercolouring artist and I love to spend time drawing in my journal, creating paintings of still life’s and abstract artwork. I am also very good at Inkscape, Gimp and Canva which I use them to create wireframes and manipulate images for new web development projects. I am also a multi-instrumentalist and love to play music at church and for my family during my leisure time. I also love reading, It helps me step into the imaginative world and experience a whole new world.

I have excellent interpersonal skills, I am able to work well with others, an active listener, a critical thinker, a problem solver, responsible and reliable. I am able to establish successful relationships among diverse groups.

I handle pressurised situations well. In demanding environments I am able to adapt and multi-task whenever new challenges are presented to me.
